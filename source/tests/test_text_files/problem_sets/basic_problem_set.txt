    problem:
        label: 1
        statement: What is the rate of change $f'$ of a function $f$ at the point $a$?
        solution:
            The derivative of $f$ at $a$, denoted $f'(a)$, is:
                $$ f'(a) = \lim_{k \to 0} \frac{f(a + h) - f(a)}{h} $$
